Nigeria

Nigeria, officially the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country located in West Africa along the Atlantic Ocean's Gulf of Guinea. It shares land borders with Benin, Cameroon, Chad, and Niger. Nigeria is known as the "Giant of Africa" due to its vast land, diverse peoples, large population, and abundant natural resources, including oil. The main ethnic groups in Nigeria are Hausa, Igbo, and Yoruba
